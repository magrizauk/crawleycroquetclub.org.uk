Hire the Lawns

Private Events

Set within the beautifully restored Victorian gardens of Worth Park, our croquet lawns offer a unique and memorable backdrop for private events, team days, and social occasions.

Whether you're looking for a team-building activity with a difference, a summer party that gets everyone involved, or a relaxed afternoon out for a group of friends, croquet at Worth Park is hard to beat.

What we can offer

We can accommodate groups of up to around 20 people on our two full-size lawns, which can be configured as four half-size courts for larger groups playing simultaneously. Our members will be on hand to introduce the game, explain the rules, and make sure everyone has a great time — no experience needed.

A typical session runs for two to three hours and includes a brief introduction to the game, some coaching to get everyone started, and supervised play. We provide all the equipment.

Ideal for

Corporate Team Days

A genuinely different team-building activity. Croquet rewards tactical thinking and encourages conversation — perfect for getting colleagues together in a relaxed setting.

Summer Parties

Hire the lawns for an afternoon or evening event. Worth Park's Victorian gardens provide a stunning setting for a summer celebration.

Group Socials

Walking groups, community organisations, U3A groups — if you're looking for an active, sociable afternoon outdoors, we'd love to have you.

Getting in touch

Private event enquiries are handled directly by the club. Please use the contact form on our main site to get in touch, letting us know your preferred date, the size of your group, and what you have in mind. We'll come back to you to discuss availability and arrangements.

Please note: private event bookings are subject to availability during the playing season (mid-April to end of September) and are dependent on lawn conditions.
